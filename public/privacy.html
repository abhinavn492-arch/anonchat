Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

AnonChat does NOT record, store, or log your video calls or text messages. Conversations are peer-to-peer and disappear when you disconnect. We do not have access to your chats and cannot retrieve them.

2. Data We DO Collect

  • IP Hash: We hash your IP address to enforce bans. We cannot reverse the hash to see your real IP.
  • Reports: When you report a user, we store: reason, timestamp, and IP hash for 24 hours.
  • Ban List: Hashed IPs of banned users and ban expiration date.

3. Cookies

We use localStorage only to remember if you agreed to the age gate. No tracking cookies, no analytics, no ads.

4. Third Parties

Video calls use WebRTC with public STUN/TURN servers. Your video data goes directly to the other user, not through our servers.

5. Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data, there is nothing to delete. To be "forgotten", just stop using the site. Ban appeals: legal@anonchat.example

6. Contact

Questions? Email: privacy@anonchat.example
